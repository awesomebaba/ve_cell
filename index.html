Shram Daan is an old concept in our Indian tradition in which people voluntarily do physical labour with the rest of Nature in the spirit of relationships. It is a necessary activity for keeping our bodies healthy and learning how to interact with Nature. Modern technological development is accelerating, but we are becoming more diseased as a result of a lack of physical labour.

VE Cell is providing an opportunity to do labour and keep yourself healthy.
